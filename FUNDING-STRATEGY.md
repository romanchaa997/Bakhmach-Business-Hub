# Funding Strategy & Capital Raising Plan

## Executive Summary

Bakhmach Business Hub is pursuing a multi-stage funding strategy to accelerate growth from $100K to $1M+ ARR over 24 months. This plan outlines funding stages, target investors, valuation strategy, and use of proceeds.

**Total Raise Target (24 months)**: $500K-$750K  
**Pre-seed Round (Now)**: $50K-$100K  
**Seed Round (Month 6)**: $300K-$500K  
**Series A (Month 15)**: $500K-$1M  

---

## Stage 1: Pre-Seed Round (Months 1-3)

### Objective
- Build MVP with core features complete
- Demonstrate product-market fit signals
- Establish early founding customer base (100+)
- De-risk execution

### Target: $50K-$100K

### Funding Sources
1. **Founder Capital**: $20K-$30K
   - Personal investment from Roman Bakhman
   - Demonstrates conviction & skin in game

2. **Friends & Family**: $20K-$40K
   - 5-8 investors at $5K-$10K checks
   - Terms: SAFE with MFN + discount (15-20%)
   - 0.1-0.3% dilution per investor

3. **Angel Investors**: $10K-$30K
   - 3-5 angels interested in co-op model
   - Terms: SAFE + equity option (0.25-0.5%)
   - Identify angels with strategic value

### Use of Proceeds
- Product development: 40% ($20K-$40K)
- Team: 30% ($15K-$30K) (contractor/part-time)
- Marketing/Growth: 20% ($10K-$20K)
- Infrastructure: 10% ($5K-$10K)

### Key Milestones
- [ ] MVP complete with 5+ core features
- [ ] 100+ founding members
- [ ] $10K MRR run-rate
- [ ] Product-market fit signals
- [ ] Team of 3-5 people

## Stage 2: Seed Round (Months 5-8)

### Objective
- Accelerate product development
- Build complete go-to-market strategy
- Scale to 500+ customers
- Establish $50K+ MRR
- Build team to 8-10 people

### Target: $300K-$500K

### Valuation Strategy
- Pre-money valuation: $1.5M-$2M
- Post-money valuation: $1.8M-$2.5M
- Seed investors: 12-25% dilution
- Key drivers: Revenue traction, team quality, market size

### Target Investors
1. **Seed-Stage VCs** (20% of raise)
   - Portfolios: Developer tools, open source, B2B SaaS
   - Check size: $50K-$100K
   - Target: 3-5 firms
   - Examples: Homebrew, Boldstart, Lerer Hippeau

2. **Impact/Social VCs** (30% of raise)
   - Focus on cooperative economics, sustainable business
   - Check size: $50K-$150K
   - Target: 2-3 firms
   - Examples: Portfolia, Katapult Impact, Next Billion Ventures

3. **Angel Syndicates** (30% of raise)
   - AngelList, Republic, SeedInvest
   - Distributed angel investor base
   - Check size: $5K-$25K
   - Target: 10-20 angels

4. **Strategic Angels** (20% of raise)
   - Industry experts, operators, founders
   - Check size: $25K-$75K
   - Target: 2-4 strategic angels
   - Benefits: Advisory board, network

### Pitch Deck Structure
1. Problem: Code/ML/Production silos costing $100B+
2. Solution: Integrated platform + cooperative model
3. Market: $10B+ TAM (optimization + ML + monitoring)
4. Business Model: SaaS + Services + Marketplace
5. Traction: 100+ users, $10K MRR, team
6. Team: Founder track record, advisors
7. Use of Funds: Product, sales, team
8. Ask: $400K at $2M valuation

### Use of Proceeds
- Team hiring: 50% ($150K-$250K)
- Product development: 25% ($75K-$125K)
- Go-to-market/Sales: 15% ($45K-$75K)
- Infrastructure/Operations: 10% ($30K-$50K)

### Key Milestones Before Seed
- [ ] Revenue: $10K-$20K MRR
- [ ] Customers: 300-500
- [ ] Product: Production-ready platform
- [ ] Team: 5 full-time equivalent
- [ ] Metrics: 90%+ user retention, 5% monthly churn
- [ ] Unit economics: LTV > 3x CAC

- Expand market presence (US + EU)

- [ ] Unit economics: 3-5x LTV:CAC, 12-18mo CAC payback

## Investor Targeting Strategy

### Profile: Ideal Seed/Series A Investor
- **Thesis alignment**: Developer tools, open source, cooperative economics
- **Check size**: Seed: $50K-$150K, Series A: $500K+
- **Portfolio**: Similar-stage companies, founder support
- **Geographic**: US-focused initially (SF, NYC, Boston)

2. **Direct outreach** (25% of pipeline)

   - Follow-up discipline

3. **Events & Conferences** (15% of pipeline)
   - Y Combinator Demo Day
   - TechCrunch Disrupt
   - Industry conferences
   - Pitch competitions

## Investor Terms & Structure

### Seed Stage
- **Instrument**: SAFE with MFN + discount
- **Discount**: 15-20% (friends & family), 20% (angels)
- **MFN**: Yes, standard terms
- **Pro-rata**: 1x in Series A for $25K+ investors
- **Board seat**: No (advisor only)

### Series A
- **Instrument**: Series A Preferred Stock
- **Liquidation preference**: 1x non-participating
- **Board seats**: 2 (investor + investor-elected)
- **Pro-rata**: 1x in future rounds
- **Dividend**: 0% (reinvest for growth)
- **Anti-dilution**: Weighted average

## Risk Management

### Funding Risks
1. **Market conditions worsen**
   - Mitigation: Build sustainable unit economics early
   
2. **Competitive threat emerges**
   - Mitigation: Strong product differentiation, community
   
3. **Execution challenges**
   - Mitigation: Experienced team, clear roadmap

### Equity Dilution
- Pre-seed: ~3-5% total
- Seed: ~18-20% total
- Series A: ~35-40% total
- Founder stake by Series A: ~55-60%
