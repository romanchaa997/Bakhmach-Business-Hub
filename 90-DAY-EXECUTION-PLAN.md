## WEEKLY EXECUTION FRAMEWORK

### Daily Standup (10am GMT+2)
```
Format: 10 minutes
Attendees: Full team
Structure:
1. Yesterday's completion
2. Today's priorities
3. Blockers/help needed
Output: Updated task list
```

### Weekly Planning (Monday 9am)
```
Format: 1 hour
Attendees: Team leads + CEO
Structure:
1. Review previous week metrics
2. Discuss week priorities
3. Confirm DRIs and deadlines
4. Risk identification
Output: Clear weekly goals
```

### Weekly Review (Friday 4pm)
```
Format: 1.5 hours
Attendees: Full team + advisors (optional)
Structure:
1. Metrics review (vs. targets)
2. Demo new features/progress
3. Team feedback & celebration
4. Next week priorities
5. Blockers & action items
Output: Meeting notes + metrics update
```

### Monthly Business Review (Last Friday)
```
Format: 2 hours
Attendees: Full team + board/investors
Structure:
1. Month review (goals vs. actual)
2. Financial review
3. Metrics deep dive
4. Learnings & retrospective
5. Next month priorities
6. Risks & mitigation
Output: Board deck + updated forecasts
```
